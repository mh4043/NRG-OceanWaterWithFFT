Ocean waves with FFT on GPU

Controls: rotate: left mouse button, move: shift + left mouse button, zoom: scroll wheel


Parameters

Resolution (N, M) *
64x64

Wave Heights Constant (A) *
3*e^-7

Wind Speed (V) *
30m/s

Wind Direction (ω) *
45°

Small Wave Cutoff (l) *
0.1m

Wave Choppiness Constant (λ)
1

* Restarts simulation, except camera
** If the animation stops while changing parameters, change the parameter again a bit.
